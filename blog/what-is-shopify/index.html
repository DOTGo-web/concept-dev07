    What is shopify & how it Work?

    Shopify is online selling platform which allow to run your own online eCommerce store with low Startups cost and very easy to manage all things with really easy user interface. Shopify provide services like marketing, shipping, payments and customer promotions tools. You can sell anything to anywhere to your customers.

    Shopify is cloud based SaaS hosted eCommerce platform so you not needs to think on software upgrade, web server upgrade or no maintenance cost. You needs to select best subscription plan as per your need and you can start selling online using Shopify with minimum cost. So, without thinking much try Shopify free for 90 days that too without credit card details.

    There are 3 types of subscriptions as shown below and you can see more information about services and features differences at Shopify site.

    Basic Shopify: All the basics for starting a new business

    Shopify: Everything you need for a growing business

    Advanced Shopify: Advanced features for scaling your business

    There are some additional plans also, Shopify Plus and Shopify Lite.

    Subscription with Shopify includes secure hosting for your store with 99.98% uptime and free SSL certificate installed, you will have also 24/7 customer support.

    Using backend / Admin panel, you can manage your store data, product catalogs, orders, customers, analytics, marketing, discounts and also you can configure sales channels facebook, amazon etc.

    You can also choose and apply free or premium shopify theme to make your store front cool and morden. You can choose template based on your store need.
