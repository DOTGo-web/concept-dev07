    What is magento theme?

    A theme part of Magento that provides a consistent look and feel to your eCommerce store. Through Magento themes, your store can get improved look for particular areas such as front website, customer dashboard and other storefront by utilizing a combination of unique layouts, styles, templates, and other image developments.

    The difference between default magento themes & custom themes

    Generally, Magento comes with two different default themes named Luma as a demo and Blank. Initially, you can use Luma and Blank to get a feel of Magento store and how does it work for your business. However, to make your store unique with amazing layouts and templates, you must try our custom Magento theme development services to get the desire look for your store.
