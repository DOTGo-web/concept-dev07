    Key benefits to choose magento development

    We have compiled a quick list of top most benefits of considering Magento development for your new eCommerce project:

    Open source

    The key benefit of Magento is an open source platform. There are two options available to choose. Magento Enterprise Edition & Magento Community Edition. Both of these platforms give permission to modify codes as per business needs.

    Multiple storefronts

    With a single install, you can manage multiple storefronts. It is very easy to add multiple shopping stores to the platform by ensuring a fascinating shopping experience.

    Easy to extend

    With Magento CMS platform, 3rd party integrations are easily manageable. You can integrate with a large number of third party services such as database applications, payment gateway, shipping tracking, logistics, point of sale etc. You will also experience with elite built-in Google tools like Google Checkout, Google Analytics, Google Base, etc.

    SEO friendly

    As Magento is open-source platform, it is powered with dozens of built-in features. Magento is fastest and easiest eCommerce platform that comes with SEO friendly feature. Apart from SEO, it also has features like Search Engine, Sitemaps, URL Rewrites, SEO friendly URLs, etc.

    Scalable

    No matter what type of online business you are dealing with. Magento cater to unconditional support as it needs. With its excellent scalable feature, it gives chance to grow your business with infinite capabilities. Magento can handle any amount of products, customers and orders without fail.

    Built as an eCommerce platform

    From the beginning, Magento is always meant for eCommerce business. It is developed for the purpose of serving eCommerce solutions. Magento is the only platform which serves excellent eCommerce solutions combining with fascinating designs and efficient features.

    Responsive

    Magento is compatible with all modern devices and so it’s responsive by default. Your site will work efficiently in all type of devices and gives amazing user experience. Your customers can easily shop online through their mobiles, iPads, tablets, desktops, or laptops.

    Payment options

    With Magento, you do not need to worry about payment gateway integration. Magento has numerous integration options with gateways such as PayPal, bank transfer, purchase order, cash on delivery, credit card, etc. So that your customers can easily commence payment transaction across the globe.

    Multilingual

    To connect with the world, you need to communicate with local language. And so, with multilingual and multi-currency features, your customers do not leave your store without placing an order. So, with this feature, you can draw significant attention in online market.

    Customizable reporting

    You will experience with a built-in reporting system in Magento. Through this, you can supervise purchase operations, sales statistics and customer activities to create future marketing goals. You can also use these data in business decisions.
