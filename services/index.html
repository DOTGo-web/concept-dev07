    Fixed price

    This is a waterfall model where cost, time, and scope of work is fixed. According to us, this type of working model is ideal for projects with no or little changes expected. We manage the entire projects by providing time-to-time progress feedbacks that lead to successful delivery.

    02

    Time and material based

    This model is idea-driven as it involves latest technologies and daily engagement activities with clients. Our qualified project manager works closely with clients to create a high-level project plan which can be delivered in smaller sprints. Pricing can be setup per day or per hour.

    03

    Dedicated hiring

    This model is easy approach for complete outsource engagement which will work well for long-term relationship and long-term projects. This model is perfect for who wish to have their own remote team for long-term projects. We identify the right resources for clients who work full-time.
