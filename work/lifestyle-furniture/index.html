    About the client

    Our client is a furniture specialist who supplies a high-quality furniture with affordable prices. Along with this, they offer excellent customer services and fascinating discounted prices on amazing products. They came to us to make new website for their company and also wanted to implement other features like shipping calendar and SEO optimization.

    The opportunity

    We have chance to deliver robust eCommerce solutions by developing responsive website using Magento 2 and WordPress. LIFESTYLE FURNITURE wanted an upgrade version of website that can stand out by delivering value to clients.

    What we did

    Working closely with LIFESTYLE FURNITURE team, we have created a unique furniture store that provide affordable furniture. To streamline checkout process, we have implemented the Shipping Calendar at checkout process using the core APIs so that it will notify available calendar date for shipping. We have also developed custom module for V12 Payment method as per the developer guide by v12finance. Lastly, to rank higher in the search engine results, we have implemented SEO and other website optimization points.
