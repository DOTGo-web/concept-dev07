    About the client

    If you are looking for pocket friendly yet stylish garden furniture then RATTAN DIRECT would be the right choice in UK. Since 2006, they have been providing beautiful furniture sets. It’s all on us to make their website look stunning by adding beautiful features & new website design. RATTAN DIRECT wanted to migrate from Magento 1 to Magento 2 by aiming to increase more traffic to their website.

    The opportunity

    To deliver great results is our opportunity and so to make it possible, RATTAN DIRECT has chosen us. They have asked us to rebuild new website by migrating from Magento 1 to Magento 2. For that, we have researched the company and identified the right way. They also wanted to integrate shipping calender & Hitachi Fixer in the back-end. To reflect company’s professionalism, we’ve started working on their needs.

    What we did

    Being an expert in Magento development services, we knew that project process is very simple and we were confident enough to deliver project in an efficient way. By migrating site from Magento 1 to Magento 2, we have relaunched the website with fresh and customizable features. For that we have implemented the Shipping Calendar at checkout process using the Rattan Direct core APIs which will show available calendar date as per shipping method. Apart from this, we have developed custom module for Hitachi Fixer in the back-end which will use to update the billing and shipping address as well as order status by selecting the dropdown options. To meet excellent speed performance, we have implemented the SEO & website optimization points with OpCache and other complex server config.
